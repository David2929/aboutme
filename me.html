David is a Certifed Public Accountant and Notary Public in the State of Pennsyvania.

His undergraduate studies were at Temple University where he received a BBA in Accounting and Finance. His graduate studies were at Philadelphia University where he received an MST in Taxation and an MBA in Business Administration.

He also serves on the Investment Committee for the Medical Mission Sisters Society.
